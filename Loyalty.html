Loyalty

Urvashi ki ek quality h loyalty. Yrr Urvashi itni sundar h or muhje se accha usko mil sakta h. Pr vo fr b nhi jati h. Me khud sochta hu ki yrr itni sundar h or muhje me kya dekha Urvashi. Pta h Urvashi k pass bhut sari choice h . Fr b vo mere sth h . Ab btao kya prove chaiye loyalty ka . Itni choice hone k baad b vo mere sth h. Toh me q Shak kru. Relationship koi sa b ho loyalty important hoti h. Kbhi b muhje doubt nhi aaya ki urvashi muhje chord k jaye ge. Kyo muhje pta h Urvashi kesi h.Ab me agr fr b shk karu to muhje se bada pagal kon h . Bhagwan itna accha gift diya h Urvashi k roop me ki me kya batu.
