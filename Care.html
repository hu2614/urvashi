Care

Yrr duniya me sb sochte h ki koi care krne wala ho . Koi toh hum se humare haal chaal puche . Insaan b janta h ki puchne se kuch hoga nhi pr mn ko accha lgta h ki yr chalo koi toh h Jo humari care krta h. Meri life me yeh Kaam Urvashi krti h. Jb hum chat krte h or me ruk jata hu toh Urvashi puch leti h ki kya hua . Vo bhale kuch nhi hota h muhje pr usko care h ki yr kuch hua toh nhi. Me khush hu ki yrr muhje b itni acchi care krne wali dost mili. Duniya me ase bhut sare log h jin ki care krne wala koi nhi h me lucky hu ki Meri care krne wali Urvashi h. Mera hi nhi Urvahsi se agr ap 5min b baat kr lete ho toh vo apki care krne lg jati h. Usko bhagwan ne bnaya hi asa h usse kis ka dard nhi dekha jata h. Yaha Insaan dusare ki care nhi krta waha urvashi ko dekho . Honeslty urvashi bhut acchi qualtiy h.


Kuch Lines
Dost k roop me care mil gayi
Care k roop me urvashi mil gayi
